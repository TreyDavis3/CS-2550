Drivers Championship

The drivers championship has 20 drivers in and each weekend during the race they fight for points. These points are awarded to the top 10 drivers with p10 getting 1 point and p1, the winner, getting 25 points.
There is also 1 point for the fastest lap but you must be in the top 10 to be awarded it. These points are added together and the driver with the most at the end of the season is the chapmion of the world.

Construtors Championship

The construtors championship is against the 10 teams on the grid. The points from both divers for the team are combined together to get a grand total to show which team had the best overall car.
